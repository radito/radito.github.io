How I Work#

I like working close to the real business flow. Before testing or building something, I try to understand what the system is supposed to protect: user trust, operational accuracy, release stability, data consistency, or production availability.

For QA work, I focus on:

  • Understanding the requirement and acceptance criteria.
  • Mapping the end-to-end user and business flow.
  • Testing both happy paths and failure paths.
  • Checking integration points where systems usually break.
  • Improving automation and monitoring where it gives real value.
  • Communicating defects clearly so they can be fixed faster.

For development work, I focus on:

  • Keeping the system simple enough to maintain.
  • Designing workflows that match real operations.
  • Making data easier to access, track, and validate.
  • Building systems that can survive daily business usage.
